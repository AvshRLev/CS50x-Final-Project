Generally a Mercury retrograde refers to an optical illusion that makes it look as though the planet Mercury changes the pattern of its movement in relation to all the stars in the night sky for a period of about 3 weeks and appears to move backwards. Actually, all planets do that sometimes but for different amounts of time and at different intervals.

In astrology the planet Mercury is associated with comunication and transportation. When it changes direction in the night sky or "goes retrograde" the general effect is that of disruption in these domains which may result in discomfort.
